WHY BECOME A MEMBER?

Swing Away Golf Studio offers a unique, fully private room and simulator powered by Full Swing for members to use 24/7! From the avid golfer or a beginner trying to learn the game, Swing Away Golf Studio is perfect for anyone who loves the game of golf! Once you become a member you will have full access to book at the facility. All members can bring guests to practice and play with.



How it works: There's no employees on site, you can schedule a time to see the studio or a free 30 minute demo by calling or emailing us! If you decide you want to sign up with us we will then schedule a time to do a simulator tutorial and get you the app that you can unlock the door with from your phone! You will then have full access to the studio 24 hours per day!



Our simulator overhead camera will give you all the feedback you need to help improve your golf game!

  • Total distance
  • Carry distance
  • Club speed
  • Ball speed
  • Club path
  • Club face
  • Ball direction
  • Launch angle
  • Shot apex
  • Smash factor
  • Backspin
  • Spin axis
